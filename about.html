UX Designer, a digital link between a company and their users.

I'm a Ux designer who takes her experience from a Bootcamp in UX/UI Design, a master of research, and a 5 years background of self-entrepreneurship in art restoration and freelance Webdesign.

This allows me to have a global vision and to combine my creativity with solid analysis and management methods in the field of digital and user experience.

I strive to base any design solution on a thorough study of the habits and needs of users and the industry for a result adapted to their needs and the intentions of a company.

Accustomed to work both in a team and alone, face-to-face and remotely, on a variety of subjects and to make the link between a client and a business expertise, I am polyvalent and I would like to integrate a team in which I could work with other digital interface design professionals in order to bring my skills of analysis, user research and progress through new challenges.
